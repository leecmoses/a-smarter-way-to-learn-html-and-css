Why I love Taos, N.M.

The sun is strong and the people are weird.

Taos is a small town in the mountains of northern New Mexico. It's cooler than the southern part of the state, because it's 7,000 feet high. Many of the residents are high, too. (But not me. I'm weird without any help.)

Some people italicize and bold too much.

I really feel that some writers use way too much bold and an incredible amount of italics when they could use far fewer of these text effects.

It's great to be able to control spacing between letters, words and lines, but, unless you're a professional designer, it's a good idea not to try to be too creative with these options.
